Dr. Youssef Al-Demerdash

Dermatology | 14 Years of Experience

About

Dr. Youssef Al-Demerdash is a renowned Dermatologist with a strong reputation for treating skin, hair, and nail disorders with care and precision. A graduate of Cairo University Faculty of Medicine, he holds advanced certifications in cosmetic dermatology and laser therapy, combining medical and aesthetic dermatology for outstanding patient satisfaction.

Qualifications

  • MD, Cairo University Faculty of Medicine – 2009
  • Diploma in Cosmetic Dermatology – 2014
  • Board Certified in Dermatology – 2012

Experience

  • Consultant Dermatologist at Cairo Medical Center, 2016–Present
    • Leads dermatology outpatient services and laser therapy unit.
    • Specializes in treating chronic skin conditions and aesthetic concerns.
  • Dermatology Resident at National Institute of Dermatology, 2010–2016
    • Managed clinical cases involving eczema, acne, psoriasis, and skin infections.
    • Gained hands-on experience in dermatosurgery and cosmetic procedures.

Clinical Interests & Research

  • Key Specialties: Cosmetic Dermatology, Laser Therapy, Acne Treatment, Psoriasis Management
